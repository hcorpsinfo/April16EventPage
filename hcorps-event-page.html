Why This Matters

Shape the future of AI — or it will shape you.

Neutrality is an illusion. The choices made today, or left unmade, will compound for decades. This isn't a tech conference — it's a reckoning.

We'll confront the fears and tradeoffs embedded in current AI trajectories, challenge inherited assumptions, and build the shared agency to co-author a positive, humanized future. We are building a movement.

Hosted by H-Corps Alliance, KQED, OurMedia, and California Community Colleges. Lunch provided.
